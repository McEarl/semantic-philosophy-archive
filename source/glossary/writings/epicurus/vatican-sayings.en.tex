\begin{sparagraph}[style=symdoc]
The \definame{Vatican Sayings} is a collection of maxims written by
\sn{Epicurus} and some of his followers, preserved in a 14th century manuscript
that was rediscovered in 1888 in the \sn{Vatican Library}.
\end{sparagraph}
